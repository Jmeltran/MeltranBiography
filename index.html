My Life Journey

Born and raised in Oriental Mindoro, Philippines, I’ve always been curious about how things work—especially when it comes to technology. My goal is to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) and turn my passion into a profession. What truly motivates me is the opportunity to use tech to solve real-world problems and improve lives. I believe the future belongs to those who innovate, and I’m excited to be part of that journey.

What truly fulfills me is creating lasting memories with my loved ones while discovering new cultures and places. I believe life is about the adventures we have and the people we share them with.
